The Boy From Jupiter

The spaceship lands on Earth. The boy from Jupiter comes out and checks the surroundings and he is amazed to see a new and strange planet to him. He doesn’t know much about humans and the earth. He decides to explore the new place. He is surprised by the way human looks but is enjoying his time on Earth.

One day he goes for a walk as usual and there he sees a girl. He doesn’t understand this feeling as he has never felt this way before. he has never contacted anyone since he landed on earth but this time it was different, and he could feel it. so, he decides to talk to a girl. He can feel his heart beating faster and faster as he approaches her. Finally, he utters the word and says hi. The girl smiles at him and says hi too. He is blushing red, and the girl could notice it.
